# KHIDMA Financial Model (Illustrative)

## 1. Key Assumptions
- Avg task value: $120
- Take rate: 22%
- Tasks per client per year: 4
- Variable cost per task: $9

---

## 2. Per‑Task Economics

Revenue per task:
- $120 × 22% = $26.40

Variable costs:
- Payments, insurance, support ≈ $9.00

Contribution margin:
- ~$17.40 per task

---

## 3. Annual Snapshot (100k Tasks)

- GMV: $12.0M
- Revenue: $2.64M
- Variable costs: $0.9M
- Gross contribution: $1.74M

---

## 4. Fixed Cost Categories
- Engineering & product
- Trust & safety ops
- Legal & compliance
- Sales & partnerships

---

## 5. Operating Leverage
- Higher density reduces support cost
- Repeat usage increases LTV

---

## 6. Growth Levers
- Increase task frequency
- Expand B2B
- Improve take rate via value add
