# KHIDMA Pitch Deck Outline (15 Slides)

## 1. Title & Vision
- KHIDMA
- The operating system for local, on‑demand labor

## 2. Problem
- Finding reliable local help is slow, fragmented, and risky
- Consumers lack trust and price transparency
- Skilled local labor lacks efficient demand access

## 3. Solution
- Two‑sided marketplace connecting clients with vetted Providers
- Fast booking, transparent pricing, insurance, reviews

## 4. Product Demo Flow
- Post task → Match → Book → Complete → Pay → Review

## 5. Market Opportunity
- Home services + errands + light commercial
- Large, recurring, fragmented TAM

## 6. Business Model
- Take rate on GMV
- Partner integrations
- Enterprise contracts

## 7. Unit Economics
- Avg task: $120
- Take rate: ~22%
- Contribution margin: ~$17/task

## 8. Marketplace Flywheel
- More Providers → Faster fulfillment → More clients → Higher earnings

## 9. Go‑To‑Market Strategy
- High‑intent search
- Retail checkout embeds
- Referrals

## 10. Enterprise Partnerships
- Furniture retailers
- Property managers
- Offices

## 11. Competitive Landscape
- KHIDMA vs Handy, Angi, Uber

## 12. Technology & Data Moat
- Local density
- Matching ML
- Trust infrastructure

## 13. Regulatory Strategy
- Independent contractor model
- Regional compliance

## 14. Financial Projections
- GMV growth
- Operating leverage

## 15. Long‑Term Vision & Exit
- Embedded labor layer
- Strategic acquisition potential
